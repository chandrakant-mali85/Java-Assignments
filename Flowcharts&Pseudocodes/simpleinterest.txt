Q.Calculate Simple Interest
 Formula:
Simple Interest (SI)=𝑃×𝑅×𝑇/100
Where:

P = Principal amount

R = Rate of interest

T = Time (in years)

Algorithm Steps:
1.Start

2.Input P (Principal amount)

3.Input R (Rate of interest)

4.Input T (Time in years)

5.Compute: SI = (P × R × T) / 100

6.Output SI

7.End
